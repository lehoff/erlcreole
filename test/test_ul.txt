You can use lists, start it at the first column for now, please...

unnumbered lists are like
* item a
* item b
* **bold item c**

* pap
** ged
** monkey
* joe
** jimmy

blank space is also permitted before lists like:
  *   item a
 * item b
* item c
 ** item c.a

* a 
** a.a
*** a.a.a
** a.b
* b
*** b..a
* c
